Storage and web presentation of the history of Irish Orienteering Championship races.

Data

Data was collected from a few sources:

  • results on orienteering.ie & www.niorienteering.org.uk
  • Past champions page on o.ie
  • Spreadsheets maintained by Marcus Pinker

    • Data generation

    A spreadhseet was used to generate the json data and can be used to generate other years which are missing. This also serves as a backup of the data outside of github. As of 2025/04/04 data collection is ongoing.

    Data quality

    Recent results are very reliable (2007 & later), and can be found on orienteering.ie. Older results are more challenging and in some cases this may be the only digital copy of them.

    Known missing data

    First names of many old results are missing, I would appreciate input into these. If you have more info on data from the below years it would be greatly appreciated.
